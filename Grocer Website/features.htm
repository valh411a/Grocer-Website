Features

Personalize your shopping list

  • Your very own self-curated shopping list

    Create a shopping list for all of your foods so that you know what exactly you need when you go on your shopping trips! Hand-pick the foods you need either from the general food database, or from the specific dietary needs lists that can help you shop smarter.


  • Scan and find out in real time what you need to know

    With the built-in barcode scanner, you can scan applicable food packaging UPC codes and find out immediately what diets the food is good or bad for, what allergies it would be bad to come in contact with, as well as add it to your personal shopping list.

  • Maintain your diet effortlessly

    Create and automatically modify a list of the foods that are intrinsic to your diet, as well as know which foods to avoid in order to keep your diet on track.


  • Shop with allergies in mind

    Know which foods to avoid with your allergies! When creating shopping lists, you can stay informed when you add an item that will be hazardous for you to eat with your chosen allergies (if any).

  • Find the items you need and the stores you want

    Keep a list of your favorite stores. Find a store that carries the food you need for your shopping list.
