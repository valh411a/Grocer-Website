What is Grocer?

Grocer is a grocery shopping companion application that allows a user to shop for the food they need for their diets, while avoiding foods that may trigger their allergies.

About us

This website helps new potential users discover Grocer™, the grocery shopping assistant app. This website also helps current users maximize the potential usage of their current account to make their shopping experiences go as smooth as possible.

Find us in your app store of choice and start shopping smart today!
