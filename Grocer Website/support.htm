Product Support

  1. Make sure your device is compatible with the app by visiting the Google Play Store page for the app. If your device is incompatible with the app, there will be a red dialog box where the install button should be located.
  2. If your device is compatible, then your next step (should the app still be unable to be started) is to visit the Contact us page so that we can assist you directly with solving the issue.
  1. To prevent this from happening to you, please remember to regularly back up your user data from the settings periodically! The app will auto-save all changes made to your data frequently, but this method may be vulnerable to issues such as unexpected device shutdown or removal of the storage device for the app.
  2. if you are missing any data that was entered recently, this may have been due to a sudden interruption of the app while user data was being saved.
  3. If all of your saved items, lists, stores, etc. are missing, first open the settings and make sure you did not previously reset the app from the settings. You will know that this was done when a red section is displayed at the top that contains the words "App has recently been reset, all user-related items have been removed."
  4. If the dialog is not displayed (it will remain for up to 48 hours after a reset), then attempt to restart the app by closing and re-opening the app.
  5. If all of your data is still missing after restarting the app, then please visit the Contact us page so that we can assist you directly with solving the issue.
  1. The barcode scanner requires permission from you in order to use the camera on your device.
  2. When starting the barcode scanner for the first time, you will receive a dialog requesting permission to access the camera on your device.
  3. Clicking 'allow' will permit usage for the barcode capturing service. Clicking 'deny' will bring you to the issue described above.
  4. To correct the issue, visit the settings app on your device and reset the permissions for this app.
  5. If the issue persists, please verify that the camera for your device is operable.
  1. The information for each food, allergy, diet, store, etc. have been gathered and displayed as best interpreted by the information available publicly for each item. If you have found information that is verified as incorrect, please visit the Contact us page and let us know.

Issue not listed here? please visit the Contact us page and tell us what problems you are experiencing.
